Blossom Village

A village
worth building

We are in the planning and design stage of something genuinely unprecedented — the UK's first Muslim eco-permaculture village. A place where families can live off-grid, grow their own food, raise their children with intention, and build the kind of community that most of us have only dreamed about.

The Vision

Not just a place to live —
a way of living

"We are not building houses. We are building the conditions for a generation of families to live as they believe — connected to the land, to each other, and to something greater than themselves."

Blossom Village is being designed from the ground up as an intentional community — one where Islamic values, ecological principles, and the ancient wisdom of permaculture converge. It is a response to a real and growing need: for Muslim families who want to live differently, who are tired of the disconnection of modern urban life, and who are ready to build something lasting.

We are currently in the planning and design phase. The vision is clear. The land is being sought. And the community — the people who will shape this place — is beginning to form. If that resonates with you, we want to hear from you.

Off-Grid & Self-Sufficient

Designed to generate its own energy, grow its own food, and manage its own water. Not as a hardship, but as a deliberate choice to live lightly and sustainably on the earth.

Rooted in Islamic Values

Built around the principles of stewardship (khilafah), gratitude, community (ummah), and care for the natural world. A space where faith and daily life are not separate.

Permaculture by Design

Every element of the village — from the layout of homes to the management of water and waste — will be designed using permaculture principles. Working with nature, not against it.

Family & Community First

Children will grow up with space, freedom, and a community around them. Elders will be valued. Neighbours will know each other. This is what community was always meant to look like.
